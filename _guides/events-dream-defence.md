## Running the game

- If your players are signing up for a live while in another PbP game, make sure they run `!savepoint` in that PbP game's dice channel and inform that DM (must run in the channel that is being paused, not in your dice channel)
- For your setup, a member of the city watch is standing by the enchanted mirrors Silverymoon is using to break into the dream world.
- Use Captain Aisling Underwood as your NPC; if you specifically would like a different one, you may, and if you'd rather have minimal NPC interaction and just have the players dive through the mirror, also grand.
- If a player dies, they are kicked out of the dream world and tumble back through the mirror; they do not suffer harm
- Any consumables used in the Dream World are still used as per normal, and summoning and class features etc work normally in the dream world

## Rewards

- We have a separate rewards sheet! Use: [Rewards Calculator: Encounterer](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1JubFamIuVg73kIUyeA9Fc9LzsLXI-T6prieBJcB0aE8/edit?usp=sharing) - Make a copy, do not edit our master copy
- XP is awarded for monsters killed by party only
- If a player dies during the encounter, they get 1/2 xp
- Gold is per encounter, not per monster, and you still get full gold even if you die
- No items! Just gold and XP for these short games
