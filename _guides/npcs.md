# NPC Staff

NPC staff provide in-character interactions for players and support roleplay scenes.

---

## Responsibilities

- Maintain minimal weekly availability to be pingable as NPCs
- Roleplay requested NPC interactions (shops, scenes, or storylines)
- Assist players in transactions or scene-related tasks

---

## Activity Expectations

- Try to be available for at least one scene per month to be considered active
- See the NPC guide channel for more specific procedures and resources
